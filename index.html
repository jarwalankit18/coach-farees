About Coach Farees

Coach Farees provides specialized UPSC Civil Services preparation focusing on Essay and Ethics (GS Paper IV) with structured online mentorship. The platform also offers dedicated UPSC and IFoS interview guidance programs, combining strategic preparation with personalised feedback.

Based in Bangalore, all programs are conducted in online mode to ensure accessibility and flexibility for aspirants across India.

Programs Offered

Essay Mastery Program

Structured framework, weekly practice, evaluation, and personalised feedback.

Ethics (GS Paper IV)

Case study practice, value-based analysis, answer structuring techniques.

UPSC & IFoS Interview Guidance

Mock interviews, personality assessment, board simulation and strategy sessions.

Mentorship & Study Lounge

Aspirants receive dedicated one-to-one mentorship, performance tracking, answer review sessions, and access to an exclusive online study lounge for discussion, peer learning, and continuous improvement.
